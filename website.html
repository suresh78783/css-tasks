Here's some information about the 2009 movie Oy!: Plot: The movie is about Uday, a wealthy young man who falls in love with Sandhya, a modest young woman. Uday is a short-term pleasure seeker, while Sandhya is a responsible loner with long-term plans. Uday discovers that Sandhya is terminally ill, but she doesn't know. He tries to make her realize her unfulfilled wishes. Cast: Siddharth plays Uday, and Shamili plays Sandhya. Director: Anand Ranga directed the movie. Reviews: Some say the movie's seamless integration of music, vibrant colors, and authentic depiction of South Indian culture make it a must-watch. Others say the movie's songs and dances are the best they've seen on Telugu screens in a long time. Running time: The movie is 2 hours and 46 minutes long.
